ACES

Ark Contract Execution Service


Write smart contracts in Solidity and deploy them to the Ethereum blockchain using Ark SmartBridged transactions.

Find Out More

Communicate directly with Ethereum using Ark SmartBridge text.

Using ACES, you can deploy smart contracts to the Ethereum blockchain using only Ark transactions and this application. Simply write your contracts or modify one of our (future) templates and call them to deployed using an Ark transaction.

Go Deeper

How It Works

Write and Save Your Contract

You can use one of our templates or write your own contracts.

Communicate to the ACES provided address

Send a transaction to the Ethereum Delegate, including the necessary Ethereum gas fee, and a SmartBridge reference to your contract code.

Wait For Response

The ACES address will return any excess Ark to your account once the Ethereum contract is deployed.

Confirm Your Contract Has Deployed

Follow the reference provided to see your contract deployed!
